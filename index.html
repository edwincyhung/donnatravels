Want to go to Japan?

Hopping on a flight to Japan is easy, but exploring the country is very challenging. Transportation can be confusing and time consuming. Authentic local restaurants are intimidating and tourist friendly restaurants are expensive and busy. Luckily, I can personally curate a complete itinerary tailored to your needs.

  • Get Started

What kind of adventure are you looking for?

Fill out my questionnaire and tell me more about your dream trip to Japan. Which cities do you want to visit? What attractions do you want to see? How long will you stay and what is your budget?

Transportation

Dozens of train and bus networks in Japan operate independently. Depending on your timing and budget, I can organize and schedule all your transportation needs from start to finish!

Restaurants

There are thousands of restaurants in Japan, but good ones are difficult to find. Depending on your tastes, I can recommend the right one for you!

Cities

Each city has many districts, all with their own distinct vibes and attractions! Whatever your interests are, there are places you should go, and places you should avoid.

Local Insight

Many destinations in Japan require careful planning. Some museums require tickets to be bought weeks in advance and some markets close as early as 3pm!

Shrines

Japan has a long history of culture around shrines but getting to them can be tricky and time consuming.

Towns

Even if you've been to Japan before, there are still lots of places to discover.

The Questionnaire

In my questionnaire, I will ask you many questions. You can answer as many as you like, to as much detail as you like. Of coruse, the more information you can give me, the more I can personalize your itinerary.

Budget

How much do you want to spend on food, accommodations, transportation, shopping, or attractions?

Time

How much time would you like to spend at each destination? Will you be up early or stay out late?

Transportation

Do you like to take your time and get lost in the city? Or would you prefer to visit as many destinations as quickly as possible?

Dates

What are the dates you will be in Japan, and which days would you like me to plan?

Group

How many people will be on the trip? Is this a family trip or with friends?

Food

What kind of food do you like or would like to try?
